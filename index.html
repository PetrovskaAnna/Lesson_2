Listening

  1. Listen and read, then answer the questions
    1. What kind of party is it?
    2. What have they got for Nigel?

    1. Nigel is very happy
    2. Nigel is not very happy

Speaking

  • Speak on spending free time by different people
    • Travelling
    • Doing sports
    • Learning
  • Ask and answer in pairs
    • Do you like parties?
    • What kind of parties do you usually have?
